Delícias da vila

Here, our passion for gastronomy is reflected in every dish we serve. Founded in 2022, we are a restaurant and pastry shop dedicated to providing the best experiences for our customers.
At Delícias da Vila, every meal is a celebration, and every customer is part of our family. Come visit us and discover the taste of hospitality!

In addition to our diverse menu, which includes irresistible savory and sweet delights, we also offer personalized catering services for events such as weddings, birthday parties, and corporate gatherings. We are committed to meeting your needs and ensuring that every event is special.

Frango à Zambeziana

Grilled chicken with coconut and other typical Zambezia spices, served with traditional sides (rice, Chima).

Picanha

Juicy grilled picanha cut, served with french fries, white rice, black beans, pineapple, and house salad.

Breaded Squid

Breaded and fried squid, crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, served with tartar sauce.

Chicken Burger

Chicken Shawarma
